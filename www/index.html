We've got what you need!

We are building an app for college students to find people nearby to eat lunch or dinner with. Since college communities can be so large and one’s vast friend group will have varying schedules, our app makes it easy to find your friends when you’re free. This way, you’ll never eat alone!

At Your Service

Creat Demand

With your location and any dishes you'd like to eat today, like Chinese, Japanese or fast food.

Get Result

Our system will automatically match person for you, who have the same demand, and lists a content for you to choose.

Send Requirement

You can see their pictures and profile in the list. Choose the one or more and offer an invitation.

Let's eat!

If he or she agrees, you can have lunch or dinner together.
